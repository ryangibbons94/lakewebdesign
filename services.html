Design

After we have gathered all of the information we need, we begin designing your website. This step usually takes about a week and we may come back to you with any questions or to get clarification. While our goal is to get it right the first time we understand that this is a process and will make adjustments based on your feedback if needed.

Our in-house designer allows us to offer design edits at any point in the process and we can even help with logo creation or graphic design.

Develop

This is where it all comes together. Our developer will take the design that was approved by you in the last step and bring it to life through code. The developer will build it in a mobile-first way, to ensure that the website is accessible and up to standards on all devices.

What sets us apart from the competitors is that we custom code everything, meaning we do not use Wordpress, Wix, or any page builders. This ensures blazing fast speeds as every line of code is important and useful.
